Before The Beginning

During the Covid period, when entire world were forced to lock themselves in their home, we got our new Home under the vast sky of Cinema.

All started with a Basic Filmmaking workshop by National Award-winning Director Pradipta Bhattacharya, our very own “Pradiptada”. Few people of various age, from different part of the world with no or very less knowledge of filmmaking form a Umbrella , where the only thing common was the love for film. And in no time it became like a family , we all became family members where mentors are like our Guardian. And slowly the revolution starts.

Different types of concept, thinking, background, with very limited equipment, all started making short films. Initially just as assignment for the workshop. But not limited to. We got carried away so much, we launched our first online festival, over Zoom. Where 300+ audiences watching our films and talking about our films. And that includes Big names from the Bengali Film fraternity. And now Covid is over. So we thought this is the best time to come out of virtual world of online Festival and here comes OFSF 3.0.

Rules And Regulations

  1. Duration : The duration of the film must be less than 30 minutes. There is no restriction on the minimum duration.
  2. The productions completed on or after 1st September 2021 are eligible for the entry.
  3. Productions that have not been released publicly will receive priority.
  4. Films can be in any language, but have to be subtitled in English.
  5. Decision for selection of any film for screening is the sole prerogative of the selection committee of the festival. No negotiation will be entertained in this regard.
  6. One filmmaker can submit any number of entries but a separate submission form should be submitted for every entry with separate entry fees.
  7. Submission fees for one entry is 200 INR.
  8. Films once selected and notified cannot be withdrawn.
  9. Stick to the deadlines, for your entry to be eligible.
  10. For each selected film there will be a Q & A session after the screening of the film with the Cast and Crew members.
  11. For promotional/publicity purposes, excerpts from any film (Max. 1min) may be used on a non-commercial basis.
  12. OFSFO 2022 reserves the right to store a copy of selected films in the festival digital archive. The films may be used for future non-commercial screenings by Our Films Collective, with prior intimidation to the filmmakers.
  13. Certificate will be provided to each of the selected films.
